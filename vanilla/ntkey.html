NTKey Log In

Welcome to the Government of the Northwest Territories' NTKey service.

From this page you can log in with an existing NTKey to change your Password, change your Recovery Questions and Answers or Revoke your NTKey.

If you already have an NTKey, please enter your NTKey User ID and Password below and select Log In. Remember that your Password contains at least one upper case letter, one lower case letter and one digit.
